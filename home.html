What Are Uterine Fibroids?

Uterine fibroids are non-cancerous growths in the uterus that can cause:

  • 01 Heavy menstrual bleeding
  • 02 Pain and pressure in the lower abdomen
  • 03 Discomfort during sex

Consultation

We connect you with expert doctors who assess your condition and recommend the right treatment.

Procedure

Frequently Asked Question

How does UAE differ from surgery?
+

UAE is a minimally invasive procedure that does not need the removal of the uterus (hysterectomy). While surgery includes physically removing fibroids or the entire uterus, UAE does not remove the uterus.

Who would be a good candidate for the UAE?
+

A good candidate is someone with symptomatic fibroids that are affecting their quality of life and prefer a minimally invasive alternative to surgery.

Is the UAE treatment painful?
+

Some discomfort or pain may be felt during the procedure, but it is generally well-managed with pain relief options.

When may regular activities be resumed after UAE?
+

Most patients are able to resume normal activities within one to two weeks after the procedure.

Are there any other treatments for fibroids besides UAE?
-

Surgical options including hysterectomy, myomectomy, and hormone therapy are alternatives.

How successful is UAE in treating fibroids?
+

UAE has a high success rate in reducing the size of fibroids and alleviating symptoms.

Will fibroids come back after UAE?
+

Recurrence of fibroids after UAE is possible but less likely compared to other treatments.
